Nearly 300 million people in India
live their day without electricity.

Our Solution

Solar Village Project started to bring a lasting impact to the lowest of the lows in our world. We find truly unreached villages that live off of $2 a day and bring them electricity. This makes a truly lasting impact on their life.

Lighting for homes so people can be safely be productive after the sun sets.

Solar power to schools so we can help educate the youth so they can break the cycle of poverty in their villages.

Empowering households to take control of their future by giving each home the ability to power their home.

We have brought hope to thousands of people who have never had the luxury of a clean light.
